About Lbrce

Lakireddy Bali Reddy College of Engineering (LBRCE), Mylavaram, Krishna District, Andhra Pradesh approved by AICTE and affiliated to JNTUK, Kakinada is an Autonomous Institution. LBRCE has been accredited by NAAC, NBA (CSE, IT, ECE, EEE& ME) under Tier-I and ISO 9001:2015 Certified. It has also been awarded 2(f) and 12(B) status and Recognized as a 'Colleges with Potential for Excellence' (CPE) by UGC. It ranks in 201-250 band in NIRF India-2020.
